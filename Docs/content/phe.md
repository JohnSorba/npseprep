# NPSE General Paper — Physical & Health Education (PHE)
## Class 6 Syllabus (MBSSE)

> Extracted from the **Physical Health Education (PHE) curriculum (Class 1 – Form 3)** — **Class 6 only**.  
> Organised by term as presented in the syllabus.

---

## Term 1

### 1. The meaning of PHE
The learner will be able to:
- Define PHE.
- Talk about and discuss the importance of PHE.
- Explain the value of PHE.

### 2. Fundamental Movement
The learner will be able to:
- Demonstrate activities in traditional gymnastics.
- Demonstrate activities in modern/traditional gymnastics.
- Talk about the value of gymnastics.

### 3. Games
The learner will be able to:
- Demonstrate basic skills in games such as:
  - Soccer (kicking, dribbling, heading, etc.)
  - Chairball (throwing-and-catching, shooting, dribbling, etc.)
  - Rounders (batting, fielding, pitching, etc.)

### 4. Hygiene
The learner will be able to:
- Discuss the causes, signs and symptoms, prevention and treatment of airborne diseases.
- Talk about disposal of different kinds of refuse.
- Discuss control of vectors and pests.
- Explain the importance of the Expanded Programme of Immunisation (EPI).

---

## Term 2

### 5. Foods and Nutrition
The learner will be able to:
- Define the terms **“nutrition”** and **“nutrients”**.
- Classify foods into **‘Go’**, **‘Grow’**, and **‘Glow’** foods.
- Talk about the importance of a balanced diet.
- Demonstrate how to make a balanced diet using inexpensive foods.

### 6. Water
The learner will be able to:
- Talk about sources of water-related diseases (e.g., river blindness, bilharziasis, etc.).
- Discuss the causes, signs and symptoms, treatment and prevention of river blindness and bilharziasis.
- Talk about typhoid — its causes, signs and symptoms, treatment and prevention.

### 7. Accidents / First Aid
The learner will be able to:
- Define **sprain**, **strain**, and **fractures**.
- Talk about ways of caring for sprains, strains, and fractures.
- Discuss the contents of a first-aid kit.

### 8. Athletics
The learner will be able to:
- Demonstrate sprinting and jumping events.
- Differentiate between **track** and **field** events.
- Discuss specifications of some field events (e.g., shot put, long jump, high jump).

---

## Term 3

### 9. The Human Body
The learner will be able to:
- Name the systems of the body (e.g., skeletal, muscular, respiratory, etc.).
- Discuss the organs of the body related to each system.
- Talk about some diseases related to the various systems.

### 10. Drugs
The learner will be able to:
- Talk about essential and non-essential drugs; useful drugs and harmful drugs.
- Discuss drug abuse and reasons for drug abuse.

### 11. Rest and Sleep
The learner will be able to:
- Discuss over-sleep, laziness, and inactivity.
- Discuss the consequences of over-sleep, laziness, and inactivity.

### 12. HIV/AIDS Education
The learner will be able to:
- Describe HIV treatment and support.
- Identify symptoms, treatment, and prevention of STIs.
