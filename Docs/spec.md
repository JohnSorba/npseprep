# Prep 6 Learning Platform – MVP

## Purpose

A front-facing educational website designed to build trust with parents, engage Prep 6 pupils (ages 9–12), and funnel users into a paid quiz and mock examination application aligned with the NPSE curriculum.

---

## Target Users

* **Primary:** Prep 6 pupils (9–12 years)
* **Secondary:** Parents/guardians

---

## Core Pages (MVP Scope)

### 1. Home Page

**Goal:** Immediate clarity and engagement

* Headline: Helping Prep 6 Pupils Succeed in NPSE
* Subtext: Learn, practise, and prepare the smart way
* Primary CTAs:

  * Try a Free Quiz
  * Explore Subjects
* Visual: Child-friendly illustration (learning-focused, not playful chaos)

---

### 2. Subjects Hub

**Goal:** Demonstrate curriculum coverage and quality

Subjects included in MVP:

* Mathematics
* English Language (Grammar & Writing)
* Quantitative Aptitude
* Verbal Aptitude
* General Paper (overview only)

Each subject page contains:

* Short subject overview
* List of key NPSE-aligned topics
* 2–3 interactive example questions
* One short free quiz (5 questions)
* CTA: Practise more inside the app

---

### 3. Free Practice Quiz Page

**Goal:** Let users experience the product

* Subject selection
* 5-question interactive quiz
* Immediate feedback per question
* End-of-quiz summary (score only)
* CTA: Create an account to access more

---

### 4. Mock Exam Overview Page

**Goal:** Introduce the paid value proposition

* What NPSE mock exams are
* How the platform’s mock exams work
* What parents receive:

  * Scores
  * Topic breakdown
  * Progress insight
* CTA: View mock exam options

---

### 5. Study Tips & Advice

**Goal:** Build trust and authority

Split clearly into two sections:

* **For Pupils:**

  * How to revise effectively
  * How to approach exam questions
* **For Parents:**

  * Supporting learning at home
  * Understanding progress and results

---

### 6. About Page

**Goal:** Establish credibility

* Why the platform exists
* Focus on NPSE alignment
* Educational mission
* Clear, honest tone

---

## Features Excluded from MVP

(To be added later)

* Full mock exam engine
* Detailed analytics dashboards
* Payment integration
* Tutor matching or live classes

---

## Technical Stack (MVP)

* **Frontend:** React.js
* **Content & Auth:** Firebase
* **Styling:** Responsive, mobile-first design

---

## Success Criteria for MVP

* Users can explore subjects without friction
* Pupils enjoy short interactive content
* Parents understand the value proposition
* Clear pathway into the paid quiz application

---

## Guiding Principle

Simple, credible, engaging. The MVP proves value — not ambition.
